About Us

We are a veteren-owned and operated organiation dedicated to building a safe, crime-free and drug-free community for veterens transitioning back into civilian life.

Our organization does not seek to make a profit off our nations heros. Instead, we pour all of our donations and rental income back into the community with new amenities and services.

Our community is comprised of single-occupency studio-style homes. We only lease to honorably discharged veterens without a record of civilian violence. This keeps our community safe enough for vets to bring their families to their new homes.

We are still in the development stage but we are very excited to announce that we are actively seeking folks who would help to build their new community.

We accept housing vouchers and encourage our villagers to explore the myriad of job opportunities in the area to work towards self-sufficiency
